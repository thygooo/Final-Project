About Our Clinic

The Norsu BSC Clinic is dedicated to providing quality healthcare services to all students and staff of the university. Our team of qualified healthcare professionals is committed to ensuring your well-being throughout your academic journey.

We offer a range of services including primary healthcare, emergency care, health education, and medication dispensing.

Our Mission

To promote and maintain the health and well-being of the university community through:

  • Quality healthcare services
  • Health education and promotion
  • Preventive care initiatives
  • Timely medical interventions
  • A supportive and caring environment
